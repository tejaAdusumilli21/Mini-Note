Troubleshooting

Having issues? Here are solutions to common problems.

Notes not saving

If your notes aren't saving:

  • Make sure you have enough storage space in your browser
  • Try clearing your browser cache and restarting Chrome
  • If the issue persists, export your notes, reinstall the extension, then import them back

Extension not opening

If Mini Note won't open:

  • Check that the extension is enabled in Chrome (chrome://extensions)
  • Try removing and re-adding the extension
  • Restart your browser

Tasks not moving between columns

If drag and drop isn't working:

  • Try refreshing the page
  • Make sure you're clicking and dragging from the task card itself
